Property Types

Property is a legal term for anything owned by an individual or entity. It can be divided into two main categories: real property and personal property.

Apartments

Perfect for those seeking a low-maintenance lifestyle, apartments and condos provide modern living spaces with shared amenities like pools, gyms, and communal areas. These are typically located in urban centers or high-density residential areas.

Single-Family Homes

Ideal for families, these detached homes offer privacy, ample living space, and often come with a yard. They are located in various neighborhoods, ranging from urban to suburban areas.

Townhouses

A great option for those who want more space than an apartment but less maintenance than a single-family home, townhouses offer a middle ground with multiple floors and often include small yards or patios.

Luxury Estates

Luxury estates are high-end, expansive properties that offer premium amenities and features. These homes are often located in exclusive neighborhoods and include luxurious finishes, large gardens, swimming pools, and sometimes private amenities like home theaters and gyms.

Vacation Homes

A vacation home is a secondary residence used for leisure purposes. These properties are typically located in desirable vacation destinations, such as beaches, mountains, or lakes, and are used for relaxation and recreation.
